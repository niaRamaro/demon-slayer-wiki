Inosuke Hashibira vs Horned Demon was a battle that took place in the Drum House. It focuses on the battle between Demon Slayer Inosuke Hashibira, against the Horned Demon.

Prologue

Inosuke is seen running through the halls of the Drum House, complaining to himself about his frustration towards being relocated once again by Kyogai. He hastily rounds a corner, slamming into the wall nonchalantly, then continues sprinting down the hall towards another corner.

Battle

Upon reaching the corner, the Horned Demon ambushes Inosuke, reaching around the corner at the last moment. The slayer hastily retreats, deftly leaping away. Slowly, the demon rounds the corner and threatens to devour Inosuke, who excitedly attacks and immediately removes both of its arms before it can finish its sentence. Inosuke then beheads the demon with his Beast Breathing: Third Fang - Devour.[1]

Aftermath

Without hesitation, Inosuke promptly uses the demon's corpse as a platform to leap off of, and bounds around the corner opposite to the one he initially was headed for, then continues onwards in order to exit the Drum House.
