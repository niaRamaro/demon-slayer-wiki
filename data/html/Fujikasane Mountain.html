Fujikasane Mountain, or Mt. Fujikasane (藤 (ふじ) 襲 (かさね) 山 (やま) , Fujikasane Yama?), is a mountain full of Wisteria blooming every season from the mountain's foot to its slope.[1]


History

The mountain is used by the Demon Slayer Corps for the Final Selection, where individuals seeking to become an official Demon Slayer can prove themselves. The examinees that pass shall be recognized and receive their own Demon Slayer Uniform, Nichirin Blade and Kasugaigarasu.[2]
