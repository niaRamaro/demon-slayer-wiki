Toyo (豊 (とよ) , Toyo?)[1] is an udon street vendor.

Appearance

Toyo is a man of average height and build, with a bald head and closed eyes.

Toyo wears a dull-yellow-and-black striped kimono, light pants, and dark shoes. His first appearance shows him smoking a kiseru.[2]

Personality

Toyo is very passionate about his udon, being visibly agitated about Tanjiro Kamado discarding his bowl without eating any. However, once Tanjiro returns and orders two bowls that he finishes quickly, Toyo is pleased and thanks him for his business.[3]

History

Toyo was born and raised in Asakusa. At some point in his life, he opened an udon shop in Asakusa, as he loves the city and wants to stay there.[1]
