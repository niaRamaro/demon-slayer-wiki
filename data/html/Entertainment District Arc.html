The Entertainment District Arc is the eighth arc of Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Synopsis

Tanjiro, Inosuke, and Zenitsu accompany the Sound Hashira, Uzui, on a mission to Yoshiwara to help search for his missing wives who were investigating rumors of demons within the district.

Battles & Events

  • Tanjiro Kamado and Nezuko Kamado vs Daki
  • Tanjiro Kamado and Tengen Uzui vs Gyutaro
  • Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira vs Daki
  • Nezuko develops her demon marks
  • Tanjiro unlocks his Demon Slayer Mark
  • The Upper Ranks are summoned to the Infinity Castle by Muzan
